SDM232 Project2——Stapler disassembly and motion simulation

更新于2022-10-14|机械
|字数总计:438|阅读时长:2分钟|阅读量:

Introduction:

This project’s main content is to disassemble the stapler, understand and identify the mechanical structure of the content, briefly analyze its assembly structure. After that, we use Solidworks software to model the whole thing, then use Solidworks motion to stimulate the motion of pressing the staple and analyze the forces in this motion.

Procedure:

  • Modeling process
    First of all, we need to consider the issue of size. We uniformly adopt the actual size to reduce the error, and the parts that need to be matched must be exactly the same as the relevant size. The second is to draw a sketch by observing the structure, and then modeling. The most important thing is to coordinate the various parts into a stapler, which can show the basic structure and function of the stapler and produce the correct visual effect. Finally, beautify appropriately to make the whole look more like a stapler.
  • Later improvement after initial completion
    Different from the last assembly, as long as the matching relationship is complete and correct, and the dimensions are basically the same, there is no problem with the structure and the task is completed. But this time we are going to do a motion analysis. During the analysis, a lot of mold-through situations appeared, which caused some structures to fail to reach the correct position. In order to solve this problem, the original sketch and model can only be modified slowly, and the initial position must be determined in order to make the interference disappear. After this, we learned how to use a new tool called Interfere Check in Solidworks.
  • Motion
    We used Solidworks motion to stimulate the motion of pressing the staple and analyze the forces in this motion. In this step, we meet several problems.
    (1) The stapler often moves in a way that is out of our intention.
    Solution: The initial status was not correct which results in wrong moving direction.
    (2) The stapler cannot clamp. So that we cannot calculate the clamping force.
    Solution: The pressing force was not big enough. The k of the spring was incorrect. We readjusted the data many times.
    (3) The magnitude of the force and the parameters of the spring
    Solution: The position of the external force needs to be confirmed, and the force must meet the conditions that can be pressed down. The spring is added between the bottom surface and the upper rod, and it also needs to be satisfied to allow force to press it down.

Results:

The connecting rod structure can save effort, which the connecting rod structure can save effort——Input 20N, output 120N, efficiency is 500%.
